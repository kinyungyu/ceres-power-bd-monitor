📚 Deal Playbook — Leading Indicators From Past Ceres Deals

Reverse-engineered from all 6 Ceres partnerships (Weichai, Doosan, Bosch, Delta, DENSO, Thermax). These are the signals that appeared before each deal was announced, ranked by predictive power.

Pathway A: Management Commentary
Ceres says "imminent" or "going well" on earnings call. Deal announced 1-3 months later. Highest probability, shortest warning.
Precedent: Delta (Nov 2023 → Jan 2024)
Pathway B: Demonstrator → Deal
Ceres deploys proof system in new region. Local manufacturer notices. Deal follows 12-24 months later.
Precedent: Shell Bangalore (2022) → Thermax (Sep 2024)
Pathway C: Broker Introduction
Third-party consultant introduces Ceres to manufacturer. 1-3 year courtship before deal.
Precedent: Intralink Seoul introduced Doosan (2016 → Jul 2019)
Pathway D: Inbound Approach
Large manufacturer approaches Ceres cold. Zero advance warning. More likely now with AI data centre demand surge.
Precedent: Weichai (evaluated multiple FC companies → approached Ceres 2018)
Hidden Signal — Broker Activity (Doosan Lesson): The Doosan deal was entirely brokered by Intralink, a UK market entry consultancy with a Seoul office. Ceres publicly credited them: "Intralink were instrumental in helping us to approach the market." First contact was 2016, deal signed 2019 — a 3-year courtship. Monitor Intralink's client list and news for Ceres/SOFC mentions. Watch for Ceres engaging similar consultants in the US or Middle East — e.g., a US market entry firm or a Gulf-region trade advisory being hired.
Intralink News Create Alert — Intralink + Ceres
Cautionary Signal — The Bosch Lesson: Bosch followed the full playbook: 18% equity stake (€90m), 250+ employees assigned, €160m factory funded, 200MW target for 2024. But they walked away in Feb 2025, saying "the market has not developed as expected." Shares dropped 42% in a day. Lessons: (1) An equity investment doesn't guarantee survival — watch for partner strategic direction shifts. (2) Government policy can change — Bosch pivoted to electrolysis. (3) Geopolitical risk — the Bosch/Weichai/Ceres three-way China JV collapsed. Monitor existing partners for any signals of strategic deprioritisation.
